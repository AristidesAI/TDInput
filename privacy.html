Privacy Policy

Effective March 19, 2026

Overview

TDInput processes sensor data entirely on your devices and your local network. No data is collected, stored, uploaded, or shared with anyone — including us.

Data Processing

TDInput reads motion, orientation, magnetometer, altitude, and health data from your iPhone and Apple Watch. All sensor data is processed in real time, transmitted only over your local Wi-Fi network to an IP address you configure, and discarded immediately after. Nothing is stored on disk, in a database, or in any persistent form.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. No personal information, no usage analytics, no crash reporting, no device identifiers, no advertising identifiers, no telemetry of any kind. The app contains no analytics SDKs and no third-party frameworks.

HealthKit Data

TDInput reads heart rate data during an active workout session on your Apple Watch. This data is forwarded over your local network as an OSC message and never stored, cached, logged, or transmitted outside your local network. HealthKit access is optional.

Local Network Usage

TDInput sends UDP packets containing sensor data to a local IP address and port that you specify. This communication occurs only on your local Wi-Fi network, does not traverse the internet, and stops immediately when you close the app or toggle streaming off.

Third Parties

TDInput does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services.

Data Retention

TDInput retains no data. The only information stored on your device is your configuration preferences (IP address and port number), saved locally. These are cleared when you delete the app.

Children's Privacy

TDInput does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.
